~~FreshBeets~~

This is the sweet beet sound board, perfect for recording all your favorite sounds, noises, and sweet beets!
Instructions: To edit or add a sound to a SoundPad, (1) click the microphone button. (2) Then click on the SoundPad you would like to edit to start recording. (3) Click on the same SoundPad when you're done recording. (4) Hit that SoundPad to play your FRESHly recorded BEET!
